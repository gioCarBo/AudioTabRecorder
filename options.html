About Microphone Permissions:
To record both tab audio and your microphone, Chrome requires explicit permission. This permission can only be granted on extension pages like this one, not in the popup.

How to use:
1. Grant microphone permission using the button above
2. Return to any tab and click the extension icon
3. Check "Include Microphone" and start recording
4. Both tab audio and your microphone will be recorded
